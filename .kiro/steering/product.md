# Product Context: KOSH

KOSH is an Agentic AI FinTech platform for gig workers and community savings.
The core goal is to transition from a standard savings app to an autonomous financial ecosystem.

**Key Upcoming Features:**
1. **Tax Trap Agent**: Automatically calculates and secures tax liabilities from wallet updates.
2. **AI Financial Coach**: Context-aware guidance using Amazon Bedrock.
3. **Community Trust Score**: A proprietary risk algorithm for community lending.
